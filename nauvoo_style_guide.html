App Style Guide

Circa 1840 Pioneer Aesthetic

Design Principles

Honest Craftsmanship

Like the trades of 1840s Nauvoo, every element should feel intentionally crafted. No unnecessary decoration—every detail serves a purpose.

Warm & Welcoming

Pioneer hospitality. The interface should feel like entering a well-kept home, not a cold institution.

Rooted in Place

Colors and textures drawn directly from Nauvoo: brick homes, limestone temple, Mississippi waters, Illinois prairie.

Timeless Legibility

Typography inspired by 1840s printing but optimized for modern screens. Elegant serif faces that honor the period.
